Getting Here

Arrival Instructions

Florisa Mountain Chalet is located at Weisslahn 9, 39050 Tiers.

Drive past the chalet (1), turn and enter the underground parking from above (2)

Enter your access code at the keypad to open the garage gate

01

Approach

When approaching, please drive past the chalet approximately 100 meters, turn, and enter the underground parking from above. It is unsafe to approach directly from the valley road.

02

Garage Entry

Drive straight to the garage entrance first, as all apartments are accessed from there. Enter the number combination you received before arrival, then press the checkmark button to open the gate.

03

Parking

Choose any available parking bay without blocking adjacent spaces. Three electric vehicle charging points are available. Please coordinate considerately with other guests.

04

Exiting the Garage

To leave the underground garage, find the switch box next to the gate and press the green button. The gate opens and closes automatically.

At Your Disposal

Amenities & Facilities

Kitchen

Fully equipped with premium appliances. Dishwasher tabs and garbage bags are provided at arrival.

Baby Comfort

Portable cribs with linen and high chairs are available. If not already in your suite, please pick them up from the cellar.

Laundry & Ironing

Washing machine, ironing board, iron, and clothes horse located in the cellar. Please return after use.

Bike & Ski Storage

Clearly marked rooms in the garage area. Please ensure doors remain closed.

Vacuum Robot

Each apartment is equipped with a vacuum cleaning robot. Fill the water tank (no cleaning agent), empty the waste water bin, and press the ON button.

Waste & Recycling

Waste separation is important in South Tyrol. Organic and residual waste go to outside bins. Glass, plastic, and cardboard go to the recycling cellar.

Winter Adventures

Ski Information

Nearest ski area: Carezza Ski Resort — a family-friendly mountain paradise.

Seilbahn

3 minutes from Florisa

Runs every 15 minutes with the unique option of standing on top of the cabin roof for an incredible panoramic view.

Parking Frommeralm

20 minutes from Florisa

Beautiful drive via the Niger Pass. Park and step directly onto the slopes. Ski rental available for approx. €51 per person. A bus also runs down to the Seilbahn.

One-day ski pass: approx. €65 per adult

Explore

Hiking & Nature Trails

Tiers via Baerenfalle to the Sesselschwaige

Starts from Florisa

Distance 4 km one way
Duration ~3h up, 1.5h down
Ascent 853 m
Highest Point 2,018 m

Walk up to the roundabout and follow route number 2. Along the way you will see clear signs. A challenging but rewarding hike with breathtaking views.

Umes to Hofer Alpl

25 min drive from Florisa

Duration ~1h one way
Difficulty Easy to moderate

Start from Umes (use Waze for navigation). We recommend stopping at the Hofer Alpl hut to enjoy the view. Check if the hut is open at hoferalpl.it
